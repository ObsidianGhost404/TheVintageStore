Shipping Policy

We ship across India with standard and express options. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of payment confirmation.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai): 2–3 business days
  • Tier 2 cities: 3–5 business days
  • Remote areas: 5–7 business days

Shipping Charges

  • Orders above ₹999 — FREE shipping
  • Orders below ₹999 — ₹99 flat shipping fee
  • COD orders — additional ₹30 handling charge

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a WhatsApp message and email with your tracking number. You can track your order on the courier partner's website.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love what you ordered. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Sale items are not eligible for return unless defective

How to Initiate a Return

Contact us on WhatsApp at +91-XXXXXXXXXX or email us at support@thevintagestore.in with your order number and reason for return. We'll arrange a pickup within 2–3 business days.

Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. For COD orders, refunds are issued as store credit or bank transfer.

Exchange Policy

We offer size exchanges subject to stock availability. Contact us within 7 days of delivery to initiate an exchange request.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how The Vintage Store collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you place an order or create an account
  • Shipping address for order fulfillment
  • Payment information (processed securely — we do not store card details)
  • Browser and device data for improving the website experience

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates via WhatsApp/email
  • To send promotional messages (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve our website and product offerings

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled by certified payment gateways and we never store sensitive financial data on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for shipping (Shiprocket/Delhivery), payments (Razorpay), and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@thevintagestore.in for any privacy-related requests.

Refund Policy

We offer full refunds for eligible items under the following conditions.

Eligible for Refund

  • Item received is damaged, defective, or significantly different from the product listing
  • Incorrect item delivered (wrong size, color, or product)
  • Item not delivered within 15 business days of the estimated delivery date

Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after order is dispatched
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items returned without original tags or packaging
  • Sale or discounted items (eligible for exchange only)

Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Online payments are refunded to the original payment method. COD orders are refunded via bank transfer or store credit.
